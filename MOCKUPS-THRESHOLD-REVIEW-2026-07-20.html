Concept 2 · "Ratify is a walk, not a view"

Same queue, presented one item at a time — the One-question grammar applied to the whole ratification backlog. A "Clear the queue" affordance (from Today or the widget) enters a focused walk: one card, verbs, next. Progress line, leave any time, order = decision weight then age. This is ALSO the voice script: on a call, the companion walks the identical queue and the app co-presents each item (§J present() directives) while spoken "confirm / skip / snooze" maps to the same PATCH verbs. Cheapest to build on top of Concept 1 (the aggregation is shared); weakest as the ONLY shape — skimming/reordering wants the flat view. Recommend: Concept 1 first, walk as increment 2.
